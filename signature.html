What is an Online Signature Generator?

An online signature generator is a tool that lets you create a digital version of your handwritten signature. Instead of printing, signing, and scanning a document, you can generate your signature online and add it directly to digital files. Our tool gives you two flexible options:

  • Draw your signature naturally with your finger, mouse, or stylus
  • Type your name and stylize it with beautiful fonts

No need to download any apps or deal with complicated design software.

Why Use a Digital Signature?

Digital signatures are essential in today’s fast-paced, paperless world. Whether you're a freelancer, student, business owner, or someone filling out forms, a professional digital signature saves time and makes a strong impression.

Common uses for digital signatures:

  • Signing PDFs, contracts, and NDAs
  • Job applications, resumes, and cover letters
  • Email footers and branding
  • School permission slips and forms
  • Creative watermarks for artists and freelancers
